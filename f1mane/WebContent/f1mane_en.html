How to play

F1 Mane is a strategy game of F1 racing

Inside the game:

  • Your pilot is in the blue cell in the table on right side of screen
  • Use Z,X,C to control the rotation of the engine
  • Use A,S,D to control the aggressiveness of the pilot
  • Use B to go to pit stop
  • Use the arrow keys(LEFT e RIGHT) to choose the path
  • Use the arrow key UP to use DRS
  • Use the arrow key DOWN to use ERS
  • Use the mouse scroll wheel for zooming
  • You can follow the strategy of the other pilots in the table

Online game:

  • Players can create and edit teams and drivers
  • A game can have up to 24 simultaneous players
  • You can create championships
  • The player can evolve with your driver and team points earned
  • You can see ranking of drivers, teams and players
  • Lots of circuits and pilots available to play
